OBJECTIVES
  • ⬡ Collect all KEYS to unlock DOORS blocking the path
  • ▶ Reach the EXIT tile to complete the sector
  • ◆ Pick up AMMO crates to gain extra bombs
  • 💣 Detonate BOMBS (1.2 s fuse) to destroy enemies in blast radius
PUZZLE MECHANICS
  • ⊙ Pressure Plates — Step on the cyan diamond tile to activate a linked door. Timed doors open briefly; one-way doors open permanently.
  • ⧖ Timed Doors (purple) — Open for a limited time after plate activation. A countdown arc shows remaining time — move fast!
  • ▷ One-Way Doors (teal) — Permanently open once the linked pressure plate is activated. Plan your route carefully.
  • » Conveyor Tiles — Auto-push you in the indicated arrow direction. Chain through corridors — you can't stop mid-conveyor.
  • ✕ Trap Tiles (red X) — Trigger an alarm that spikes the alert level. Avoid them when possible; pick up keys carefully.
  • ▬ Laser Beams (red) — Block paths while the emitter (wall tile with glowing core) is active. Detonate a bomb near the emitter to disable it temporarily.
  • Time slows down when you are near an enemy's vision cone — use it to think
  • Enemies do not detect you instantly — a buildup meter (orange→red bar above them) fills while they see you; leave their cone before it fills
  • Detection needs vision range + vision angle + clear line of sight — walls block it
  • Meter at full → enemy enters ALERT (red cone) and chases your position at speed
  • Escape the chase: break line of sight and stay hidden — the enemy eventually switches to SEARCH (orange cone)
  • In SEARCH the enemy walks to your last known position, scans briefly, then returns to patrol
  • ⚠ DANGER warning pulses on screen when detection risk is very high
  • A red vignette intensifies around the screen as threat increases
  • A 1.5 s grace period prevents detection at the start of every level
  • Bombs permanently destroy enemies in their blast radius — wreckage stays on the map
ENEMY STATES
  • PATROL (yellow cone) — Moving between waypoints, pausing briefly at each turn
  • SUSPICIOUS (orange cone + ?) — Player spotted; meter filling; keep moving to escape
  • ALERT (red cone + !) — Fully detected; chasing your last known position
  • SEARCH (orange cone + ?) — Lost you; heading to last known spot then scanning
  • DESTROYED — Bomb wreckage; permanently inactive
ENEMY TYPES
▪ PATROL UNIT (orange)
MEDIUM SPEED  |  STANDARD CONE  |  MEDIUM RANGE

The standard guard robot. Patrols fixed waypoints with a forward-facing amber vision cone. Moderate speed and detection range. The most common enemy type — duck behind walls or time your moves when it faces away from you.

▪ SCANNER UNIT (blue)
SLOW SPEED  |  SWEEPING WIDE CONE  |  SHORT RANGE

A surveillance bot with a wide sensor cone that oscillates ±60° side-to-side as it patrols. Its sweep is independent of movement direction, making it unpredictable. Short range but very hard to sneak past — wait for the sweep to pass before advancing. First encountered in Sector 3.

▪ HUNTER UNIT (crimson)
HIGH SPEED  |  NARROW LASER BEAM  |  LONG RANGE

A fast pursuit robot with a long-range razor-thin vision beam. It moves significantly faster than other units and can spot you from across the entire room. Its distinctive diamond-shaped body and elongated red laser eye make it easy to identify. Stay completely out of its direct line of fire. Appears from Sector 6 onwards.
